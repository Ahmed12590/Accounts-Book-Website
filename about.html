Accounts Book Limited is a growing mature company focused on business professional consultancy. Starting with accounting and booking services we can provide every service you need including ISO Management and Trade Finance. Our team of accountants is the main multicultural asset of the company. The diversity, knowledge, and practical experience is what makes you offer you proactive solutions for your business.

We pride ourselves by being active in our professional communities to stay current with the latest legislation and regulation changes. This means we offer you advice based on the latest law in practice. Working with us means you don’t have to stay up to date with the changes and can focus on your company’s work. Stress-Free.


Even if we can offer all our services online we believe in a hands-on approach. As our company is growing we’re opening new branches that are closer to you. This means we can have a real conversation about your company’s needs, challenges, and ways we can accommodate that.
